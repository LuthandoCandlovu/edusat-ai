## ✦ WHAT IS EDUSAT AI? ✦

<img align="right" width="380" src="https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api/pin/?username=LuthandoCandlovu&repo=edusat-ai&theme=midnight-purple&bg_color=020b18&border_color=00f5d4&title_color=00f5d4&text_color=c9d1d9&icon_color=FFD700"/>

<br/>

**EduSat AI** is a next-generation, AI-powered platform that fights the education crisis in **Eastern Cape, South Africa** — one of the most underserved regions in the country.

Using **machine learning**, **satellite connectivity**, and **real-time data analytics**, it empowers teachers, principals, and district officials to **predict dropout risk before it happens** and act with precision.

> *🛰️ Built for schools where internet is a luxury. Designed to work anyway.*

<br clear="right"/>

## 🌟 CORE FEATURES

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🔮 Risk Prediction Engine
> **99.8% accuracy** — identifies students at risk of dropout or failure weeks before it's too late, giving educators time to intervene.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📋 AI Intervention Plans
> **One click** generates personalised, actionable intervention strategies tailored to each student's profile.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📊 Policy Simulator
> Simulate the **real-time impact** of policy changes before they're implemented — evidence-based decisions, not guesses.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🗺️ District Analytics
> **Interactive heatmaps** across all 8 Eastern Cape districts — spot patterns, allocate resources, and track improvement.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td colspan="2" align="center">

### 🛰️ Starlink-Ready Offline-First Design
> **Works without internet.** Syncs automatically when connectivity is restored via Starlink satellite — because rural schools can't wait.

</td>
</tr>
